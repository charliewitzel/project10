About Me

Hey there! I'm a 35-year-old transmasculine/non-binary individual, and I'm thrilled to welcome you to my world of creativity and passion. Aspiring web developer and a proud student enrolled in the prestigious Correlation Ones software development program, I'm constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the digital realm.
Beyond the world of coding, I immerse myself in the art of gender-bending and transformation as a seasoned drag king. For a decade, I've graced stages as iconic horror personas like Pennywise, Jason Voorhees, and Chucky, captivating audiences with my unique performances.
Photography is another integral part of my life. From capturing the energy and vibrancy of drag shows to preserving cherished memories through wedding and engagement photos, I strive to freeze moments in time, allowing emotions and stories to live on forever.
In my Etsy shop, you'll discover my artistic flair firsthand. Hand-painted patches celebrating LGBTQIA+ pride and reflecting on political matters are among the treasured items I offer. Additionally, I craft custom punk vests, each piece infused with a touch of rebellion and self-expression.
Originally hailing from a small town in Southern Illinois, I now call the vibrant city of St. Louis, MO, my home. When I'm not immersed in my creative endeavors, you can find me working at Amazon, where I've been part of the team for the past seven years.
Join me on this journey as I continue to break barriers, explore new horizons, and embrace the beauty of diversity. Together, we'll celebrate art, identity, and the power of technology to shape our world for the better. Let's connect and create something extraordinary!

Javascript

My journey through JavaScript has been an enlightening adventure, equipping me with the skills to build dynamic and interactive web experiences. In the initial lessons, I learned about JavaScript's origins and benefits, setting up a basic development environment to begin coding. Understanding variables, data types, and commenting enabled me to lay the foundation for writing efficient and organized code. Diving deeper, I explored various control structures, from conditional logic to loops, while grasping the concepts of "truthy" and "falsy" values. I delved into the world of functions, mastering the creation of different types, handling parameters, arguments, and understanding the concept of scope. Arrays and objects became powerful tools to store and manipulate data, enhanced by built-in properties and methods. I honed my skills by creating interactive elements like a dark/light mode toggle and a simple image slider, demonstrating my proficiency in JavaScript. Working with the Document Object Model (DOM) and the Browser Object Model (BOM) allowed me to manipulate and traverse elements, manage styles, and handle events. Using the Intersection Observer API, I implemented efficient ways to interact with elements that enter or exit the viewport. JavaScript modules enabled me to organize code effectively, promoting maintainability and scalability. With client-side form validation and browser storage, I built interactive web apps, empowering users to input and persist data seamlessly. Exploring external libraries like jQuery and d3 expanded my toolkit, simplifying DOM manipulation and event handling while adding captivating animations and effects to my projects. As I conclude my JavaScript journey, I feel confident in my ability to create engaging, user-friendly, and efficient web applications. This experience has been invaluable, and I'm excited to continue my journey in the ever-evolving world of web development.

SilasSilence

Step into the mesmerizing world of Silas Silence, a non-binary transmasculine drag performer who weaves horror and gender-bending artistry into captivating performances. Known for their spine-chilling portrayals of horror icons like Freddy, Jason, and Chucky, Silas Silence has taken the stage by storm.
